Server Rules



Rules

Trading

All loot within trade zones are only allowed to be bought, looting will be counted as stealing.

Selling outside of the trade zones is only possible with a 'Handheld Receiver'. Selling in the wild costs 1 '9V Battery' and must be dropped after the sale.


Theft

If a player is caught stealing, a bounty will be placed on their heads. This bounty can be claimed in the 'Rewards' tab.


Murder

If a player murders another player, a bounty will be placed on their heads. This bounty can be claimed in the 'Rewards' tab.
